Black Swan Event Interception (First Batch)

We systematically identify and prevent the following risks (more coming soon):

  • Volcanic ash, sandstorms
  • Icing/snow, hail
  • Wind shear (microburst, wake, mountain downslope)
  • Runway water/pits
  • Clear air turbulence, thunderstorms
  • Pollution environments (plants, agriculture, pollen, bird strikes)
  • Airport construction dust (no water suppression, cement mixing dust, exposed materials, tool FOD risks) — mandatory enclosed/covered/walled
  • Runway marking confusion (center dashed vs edge solid lines, no entry numbering) — strict ICAO standards + AI enhanced display
